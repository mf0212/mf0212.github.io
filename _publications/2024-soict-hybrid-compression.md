**Authors:** **Minh-Loi Nguyen\***, Long-Bao Nguyen\*, Van-Hieu Huynh\*, Trung-Nghia Le.

**Venue:** 11th International Symposium on Information and Communication Technology (SoICT 2024). **Published.**

[Paper (DOI)](https://doi.org/10.1007/978-981-96-4282-3_5)

A hybrid neural-compression pipeline that interleaves structured pruning with quantization-aware fine-tuning, with Mixture-of-Experts layers preserved as a capacity reserve. The work shows that the order and granularity of compression steps matters as much as their individual aggressiveness — yielding substantially smaller models without the accuracy collapse that follows naive sequential compression.
