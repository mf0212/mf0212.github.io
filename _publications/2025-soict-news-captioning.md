**Authors:** **Minh-Loi Nguyen\***, Xuan-Vu Le\*, Long-Bao Nguyen, Hoang-Bach Ngo, Trung-Nghia Le.

**Venue:** 12th International Symposium on Information and Communication Technology (SoICT 2025). **Accepted.**

A retrieval-augmented captioning system that grounds news-image descriptions in real, evidence-traceable facts. Hierarchical multimodal retrieval first narrows the candidate event, then surfaces supporting entities and quotes; the captioner conditions on this evidence rather than hallucinating from pixels alone. Built around the EVENTA Grand Challenge benchmark at ACM Multimedia 2025.
