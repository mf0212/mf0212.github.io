**Authors:** Van Duong-Thi-Thanh, Binh Truong-Quang, Phu Tran-Nguyen-Trong, Mai Le-Phuong, Phu Truong-Thien, Dung Lam-Quoc, Thong Dang-Vu, **Minh-Loi Nguyen**, Vu Le-Thuong.

**Venue:** *Diagnostics*, vol. 14, no. 6, p. 633, 2024. **Published.**

[Paper (DOI)](https://doi.org/10.3390/diagnostics14060633)

A dynamic, web-deployed nomogram that estimates the risk of MRSA infection in pneumonia patients from routinely available clinical features. The tool is designed for bedside use: clinicians enter what they have, the model returns a calibrated probability and the variables that drove it. The contribution focuses on usable, transparent risk modeling rather than chasing the last point of AUC.
