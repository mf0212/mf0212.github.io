**Authors:** **Minh-Loi Nguyen\***, Quang-Khai Le\*, Tam V. Nguyen, Minh-Triet Tran, Trung-Nghia Le.

**Venue:** IEEE Transactions on Human-Machine Systems (THMS). **Under Review.**

[arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2506.21832)

TaleForge weaves text, illustration, and voice into a single interactive loop for personalized storytelling. The system tracks character state and reader preference across turns, so generated continuations stay consistent with both the protagonist and the audience. The contribution targets the gap between one-shot story generators and tools that can sustain a long-form, child-led narrative session.
