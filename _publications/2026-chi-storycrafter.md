**Authors:** Xuan-Vu Le\*, **Minh-Loi Nguyen\***, Khanh-Duy Le, Minh-Triet Tran, Trung-Nghia Le.

**Venue:** ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems (CHI 2026). **Published.**

[Paper (DOI)](https://doi.org/10.1145/3772363.3798778)

A graph-based co-creative AI system that helps parents re-author bedtime stories by exposing the underlying narrative as an editable event graph. By making characters, settings, and causal events first-class objects, StoryCrafter lets caregivers steer generation with intent rather than fight with a free-form prompt. The work argues that explicit narrative structure — not larger models — is what makes co-creation feel like collaboration.
