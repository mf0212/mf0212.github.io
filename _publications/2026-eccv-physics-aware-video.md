**Authors:** **Minh-Loi Nguyen\***, Xuan-Vu Le\*, Thanh-Toan Do, Tam V. Nguyen, Minh-Triet Tran, Trung-Nghia Le.

**Venue:** European Conference on Computer Vision (ECCV 2026). **Under Review.**

A video-generation pipeline that plans before it renders. An agentic planner builds a scene graph with explicit physical constraints — masses, contacts, trajectories — and a graph-guided optimization loop steers the diffusion backbone so that motion respects what was planned. The result is video that is controllable and physically plausible without retraining the base model.
