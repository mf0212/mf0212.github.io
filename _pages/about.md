## Research Interests

My research centers on **grounded, physics-aware generative modeling** — building video and multimodal systems that reason over explicit scene graphs, causal structure, and physical constraints before they generate, so that outputs are controllable, auditable, and faithful to the world they depict. Around this core I work on:

- **Human-AI co-creative systems** that mediate user intent through graph-based event representations (CHI 2026, UIST 2026, THMS).
- **Generative video** with agentic planning and graph-guided optimization (ECCV 2026).
- **LLM alignment for high-stakes, low-resource settings** — GRPO, DPO, RLHF, and PEFT methods (LoRA, QLoRA, DoRA) applied to clinical safety and medical reasoning at Trivita AI.
- **Efficient AI** — hybrid neural compression combining pruning, quantization, and Mixture of Experts for edge deployment (SoICT 2024).

A recurring theme in my work is the bet that *explicit structure, not scale alone*, is what turns generative AI into a safe and genuine collaborator.
