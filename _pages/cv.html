Research Interests

My research centers on grounded, physics-aware generative modeling — building video and multimodal systems that reason over explicit scene graphs, causal structure, and physical constraints before they generate, so outputs are controllable, auditable, and faithful to the world they depict. Around this core, I work on human-AI co-creative systems that mediate user intent through graph-based event representations, and on LLM alignment for high-stakes, low-resource settings (RLHF, GRPO, DPO, reward shaping). A recurring theme across my work is the hypothesis that explicit structure, not scale alone, is what turns generative AI into a safe and genuine collaborator.
